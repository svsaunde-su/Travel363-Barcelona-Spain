Looking for More Things to Do?

Then look no further! Below is a list of awesome acitivites for you to do in Barcelona, Spain.

Day Trips

Tibidabo Amusement Park

An amusement park on a mountain overlooking Barcelona.

Montjuïc

A hill that has many attractions such as museums, gardens and a castle.

Montserrat

A multi-peaked mountain range home to Santa Maria de Montserrat, a Benedictine monastery.

Casa Milà or La Pedrera

A home once owned by the infamous architect Atoni Gaudi and it’s name translates to “the stone quarry".

Nightlife

Opium

Beachside lounge, bar, restaurant and club. Best nights are Friday, Saturday and especially Loco Lunes (Mondays)!

Shôko

Beachside lounge, bar, restaurant and club. Great music all the time!

Bling Bling

Club with two main rooms: one that plays all of the popular hits and the other plays strictly Spanish music!

ICEBarcelona

Beachside ice lounge decorated with sculptures entirely carved by hand and drink in below freezing temperatures!

Why Montserrat?

It is perfect for all members of the family. This day trip will be full of adventure, nature and education. From the sceneic train ride to the mountain range to the thrilling cable car ride up the mountain, Montserrat It is well known as the site of the Benedictine abbey, Santa Maria de Montserrat, which hosts the Virgin of Montserrat sanctuary; one of the Black Madonnas of Europe.
